# Utility Commands

Utility commands to help you along your sky journey.

## `/seasonal-calculator`

**Options**:

- `<candles>` - The amount of candles you currently have
- `<dailies>` - Have you completed today's daily quests?
- `[season-pass]` - Do you have the season pass?

Helps you calculate the required candles to finish the season. Depending on the items you have already acquired and your current candle count, it tells you how many days until you finish the season or if itâ€™s not possible.

**Usage**:

- `/seasonal-calculator candles:45 dailies:true`

## `/help`

**Options**:

- `[command]` - Get help for a specific command

The bot's help menu.

**Usage**:

- `/help` (shows embeds with all available commands)
- `/help command:shard` (shows help menu for the shards command)

## `/utils`

**Subcommands**

<br />
<ul>
<li>1. `timestamp` - Gives you a Discord-formatted UNIX timestamp for a given time/date</li>

**Options**

- `<time>` - The time to convert to UNIX (format: `HH mm ss`, e.g., `04 32 44`)

  <Callout type="info">The format `HH:ss:mm` is not supported because for some reason Discord formats `:xx:` into a custom emoji format (`<:name:id>`) if it matches any emojis accessible to the user. This is a workaround to prevent that.</Callout>

- `[timezone]` - The timezone to use for conversion (format: `Continent/City`, default: `America/Los_Angeles`)
- `[date]` - The date to convert (format: `DD`)
- `[month]` - The month to convert (format: `MM`)
- `[year]` - The year to convert (format: `YYYY`)

**Usage**:

- `/utils timestamp time:02 07 44`
- `/utils timestamp time:04 22 30 timezone:Asia/Kolkata`

<li>2. `changelog` - Shows the bot's changelogs</li>
<li>3. `botinfo` - General information about the bot and its configurations for users/servers</li>
<li>4. `contact-us` - Reach out to us for any queries you might have with this command</li>
</ul>

## `/language`

**Subcommands**

<br />
<ul>
<li>1. `set` - Set a language for yourself or the server</li>

**Options**

- `<category>` - The category to set the language for ("Server" | "User")
- `<language>` - The language to set

<Callout type="info">[Help us](/pages/translating) support more languages by translating the bot.</Callout>
<br />

<li>2. `remove` - Remove a set language</li>

**Options**

- `<category>` - The category to remove the language for ("Server" | "User")

</ul>
