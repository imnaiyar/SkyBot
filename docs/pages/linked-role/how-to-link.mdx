Before we go on, SkyHelper as Linked Role connection must be enabled in your server. If you haven't done that yet, you can follow the steps [here](/linked-role/how-to-enable).

How to get the Linked Role?

- **Step 1:** Open the server settings.
- **Step 2:** Navigate to the `Linked Role` tab.
- **Step 3:** Click on the `Role` that has been linked with SkyHelper.
- **Step 4:** You'll be redirected to the bot's dashboard where you'll be authenticated with Discord.
- **Step 5:** Once authenticated, you'll be prompted to set your preferences.
- **Step 6:** After setting your preferences for Sky Profile, submit nd get back to discord.
- **Step 7:** If your preferences meet the requirements set by the server admin, you'll be able to get the role. Click `Finish` and done. Open your server profile,and scroll down to see.

<Callout type="info">
  If you need to update any information about preferences/SkyProfile, simply visit
  [dashboard](https://dash.skyhelper.xyz/user/linked-role) again and it should be reflected on discord.
</Callout>
